Introduction

One of my main ambitions in life is to travel and experience a widevariety of cultures around the world. It is so easy nowadays to go online and watch a video or read a blog about a hotspot for travelers and feel like you have been immersed. The reality is that what you may see there is not as glamorious as it is protrayed. Plus, there are places off the beaten path that are arguably more in tune with the cultures that raised them. I want to learn and understand the perspectives of those who have lived drastically different lives than me. I do not want to take the easy route by going to some stereotypical destination for any traveler. I am open to travel with company.
